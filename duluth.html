Discover Duluth — My UMD Home

Why I came to UMD, what I love about Duluth, and some favorite campus moments.

Why I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because it felt like the right fit both academically and personally. While exploring majors, I discovered Consumer Insights & Analytics in UMD's catalog — and it immediately clicked with me. The program aligned perfectly with my interests in people, data, and storytelling. UMD was also closer to home than Madison, which made the decision both exciting and practical.

What I love about Duluth & the surrounding area

Duluth keeps surprising me with new things to explore—festivals, beaches, trails, cozy shops, and great local restaurants. Coming from a smaller town, I appreciate having more options while still being surrounded by nature. The trees, the lake, and the dramatic changing seasons make this city feel especially refreshing.

Personal highlights

I love the small, lively moments UMD offers, like the Friday-night events in EF2 or the Kirby Program Board events (trivia, bingo, tote-bag painting). They're fun, welcoming, and often send me home with a soda or snack to enjoy the next day. I also really appreciate how the whole campus is connected. On colder days I can get everywhere I need without stepping outside.

Sometimes I'll wander into random club events and end up having a blast. Anything from country swing nights to community tie-blanket service projects. Little conveniences like a short drive to Target make campus life feel practical and comfortable while I focus on classes and friends.
